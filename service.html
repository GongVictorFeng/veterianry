Grooming

Grooming is not only about appearance, but for good physical health too! Certain breeds should be groomed by a groomer every 6-8 weeks depending on the breed. A pet left with severe matting may have unseen hotspots, growth, ticks and other unwelcome critters under the thick pelted surface. We can advise you on the best haircut for your lifestyle and show you how to care for his/her coat between grooming to ensure that bad hair days are a thing of the past. A complete groom includes: a shampoo, massage, fluff drying, the cut of your choice, ear cleaning, trimming of hair in the paw pads, nail clipping, expressing of your dog’s anal glands upon request, and a complimentary bow or bandana for a final touch.
